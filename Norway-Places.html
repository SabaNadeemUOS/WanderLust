Restaurants

under restaurant

Having dinner at five metres below sea level. "Under" in Lindesnes is Europe's first and the world's largest underwater restaurant.

Cornelius seafood restaurant

Cornelius is one of Norway's best seafood restaurants. It is situated right by the sea on a small island with spectacular views of the fjord, mountains, skerries, and passing boats and ships.

The Svalbard Islands

here are three main seasons in Svalbard: Polar Summer, Northern Lights Winter and Sunny Winter. Each season has its unique charms and characteristics.

Tromso

Citybreak or the outdoors? Why choose when you can have both? Tromso offers a vibrant city centre, surrounded by raw nature. Here you can be enchanted by the northern lights in autumn and winter, and enjoy long days in the summer when the sun does not set.
